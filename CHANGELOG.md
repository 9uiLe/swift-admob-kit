## [0.1.0] - 2026-07-11

### Added

- MainActor-isolated `AdMobClient` for SDK startup, consent, loading, presentation, and reloads.
- Host-defined `AdSlot` and injected production unit configuration.
- Fail-safe automatic environment resolution with internal Google demo IDs.
- SwiftUI adaptive banner support.
- Interstitial, rewarded, and rewarded-interstitial support.
- Optional App Tracking Transparency authorization and UMP privacy options.
